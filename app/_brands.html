Whether you use Creators Create in campaigns or continuously, its purpose is to connect your audience with your brand by providing a fun & creative experience.

How it works

1. Choose an artist & product that fits your brand

For every campaign we choose a designer who's story & style fits your brand. We have a large network of designers and can help you find the right one. An artist delivers a set of designs which can be used to customize products.

Creators Create is about enabling people to be creative with the help of a real artist. For example. MTV collaborated with illustrator Musketon.

2. Define your campaign parameters

Depending on your KPI's we help you define the different parameters for your campaign.

  • Campaign as an incentive to buy your product Customers receive an unique code when they buy your product online or in a store. These codes can be used to order customized products on Creators Create.
  • Grow your Facebook page We integrate a voting contest using Facebook login and connect users with your Facebook page.
  • Gather data We help you to optimize the platform and gather the customer data you need. For example: email addresses to increase your e-mail marketing database.

3. We set up & launch your campaign

We bring everything together and launch your campaign on the Creators Create platform by integrating your corporate identity or totally white labeled with a custom URL.

Follow your campaign in real-time, identify the greatest traffic drivers and interact with your customers. With the build in sharing tools it is easy to spread the campaign on your social media channels.
